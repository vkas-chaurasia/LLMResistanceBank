Salmonella contamination of flocks was more frequent when (i) the layer house was easily accessible (RR=3.5, CI 95%=0.5, 25; p=0.1), (ii) the buildings were not disinfected before pullets were loaded (RR=2.7, CI 95%=0.4, 16.9, p=0.2), (iii) ventilation was poor (RR=2.6, CI 95%=1.03, 6.5; p=0.08), (iv) dead hens were stored on the farms for at least 1 week (RR=2.6; CI 95%=1.0, 6.5; p=0.08), (v) we observed living or dead rodents inside the henhouse on each sampling visit (RR=2.6; CI 95%=0.8, 7.6; p=0.08), and when (vi) cages were dry cleaned only before pullets were loaded (RR=2, CI 95%=0.7, 5.3; p=0.2) (Table 3).
